Our Story.

Biofics is a quickly growing venture providing out of the box solutions to its clients in Waste Management. We help you strategize your environment goals and make your facility green and waste-free.

Biofics has been recognized in the market for its innovative solutions and services in waste management, in high profile organizations such as the Gateway Hotel (TAJ Hotels), the Grand Bhagwati Hotels & Banquets, Navin Fluorine International Limited (Arvind Mafatlal Group), JHM Hotels among others.

What We Do.

Biofics offers a full range of Waste management services to suit any type of facilities and in an economical budget. All type of waste generated at a facility either organic or inorganic waste is handled & processed with our proprietary fast composting technology resulting in organic green fertilizer for landscaping needs. Our technology along with our well trained staff will ensure your environment is waste free, healthy, pleasant, safe and green.
